The Spark Of Innovation

In 2001, the spark of ceramic tiles distribution ignited within us, leading to the inception of Mayur Ceramic. Our journey started in a modest 500 sq. ft. space, driven by a grand vision to redefine interior aesthetics.

By 2023, we expanded into a massive 8000 sq. ft. showroom on Silvassa Road. We empower our customers to immerse themselves in the world of luxury tiles, marbles, and sanitary wares.
